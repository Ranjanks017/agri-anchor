Cactus

Price: $4

Cacti (plural for cactus) are a family of plants that are well adapted to hot, dry environments.

Family: Cactaceae

Native Habitat: Mostly native to the Americas, particularly to regions with desert climates, although a few species can be found in other parts of the world.

Structure: Cacti are typically succulents, meaning they store water in their stems. This gives them their distinctive, often thick and fleshy appearance.

Spines: Most cacti have spines, which are modified leaves. These spines provide protection against herbivores and reduce water loss.

Flowers: Cacti are known for their large, showy flowers, which can be quite colorful. Their blooms often appear in spring or summer and are usually quite short-lived.

Roots: Cacti often have shallow, wide root systems designed to absorb water quickly after rainfall.
